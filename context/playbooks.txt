[PLAYBOOK: BRUTE FORCE ATTACK]
- Criteria: Multiple failed login attempts.
- Action: Disable the user account and trigger a password reset.

[PLAYBOOK: UNUSUAL GEOLOCATION]
- Criteria: Login from a country not on the approved whitelist.
- Action: Force Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) challenge as per the Unusual Geolocation Playbook due to the brute force attempt.

[PLAYBOOK: MALWARE DETECTION]
- Criteria: Known malicious hash found on endpoint.
- Action: Isolate the host from the network immediately.
